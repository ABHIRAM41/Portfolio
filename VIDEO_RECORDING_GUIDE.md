# 3D Portfolio - Video Recording Guide

## 📹 Recording Requirements

Create a demo video showcasing your 3D portfolio with the following specifications:

### Duration
- **Minimum**: 2 minutes
- **Maximum**: 5 minutes
- **Ideal**: 3-4 minutes

### Format Requirements
- ✅ **Show your face** (webcam overlay or split screen)
- ✅ **Show your computer screen** (the deployed portfolio)
- ✅ **Speak in English** throughout the demo
- ✅ Use Loom or similar screen recording tool

---

## 🎬 Suggested Demo Flow

### 1. Introduction (30 seconds)
- Introduce yourself
- Brief overview of what you built
- Mention the tech stack (Next.js, React Three Fiber, etc.)

### 2. Landing Page & 3D Elements (45 seconds)
- Show the hero section with 3D floating shapes
- Demonstrate the particle field animations
- Highlight the smooth animations and gradients
- Show the scroll indicator animation

### 3. Navigation & Sections (90 seconds)
**About Section:**
- Navigate to About section
- Point out the glassmorphism effects
- Highlight key metrics (40% velocity increase, 99.9% uptime, etc.)

**Experience Section:**
- Show the timeline visualization
- Explain your role at Vectorsoft
- Mention key achievements

**Skills Section:**
- Demonstrate the skills grid
- Show hover effects
- Point out the skill categories

**Projects Section:**
- Show featured projects
- Click on live demo links if available
- Explain the technologies used

**Education Section:**
- Briefly show educational background

**Achievements Section:**
- Highlight competitive programming stats
- Show the animated stat cards
- Mention LeetCode streak, HackerRank ratings

**Contact Section:**
- Show the contact form
- Demonstrate form interactions

### 4. Responsive Design (30 seconds)
- Open browser DevTools
- Show responsive design on different screen sizes
- Demonstrate mobile view

### 5. Performance & Closing (30 seconds)
- Mention performance optimizations
- Show smooth 3D rendering
- Thank viewers and provide your contact info

## 🛠️ Recording Tools

### Recommended: Loom
1. Go to [loom.com](https://www.loom.com/)
2. Sign up for free
3. Download desktop app or use browser extension
4. Start recording with webcam + screen

### Alternative Tools
- **OBS Studio** (Free, advanced features)
- **Zoom** (Record meeting with yourself)
- **QuickTime** (Mac only, + webcam app)
- **Windows Game Bar** (Win + G on Windows 11)

## ✅ Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Portfolio is deployed and accessible via public URL
- [ ] Test all links (GitHub, LinkedIn, LeetCode, project demos)
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs and applications
- [ ] Check webcam positioning and lighting
- [ ] Test microphone audio quality
- [ ] Prepare talking points notes
- [ ] Do a quick practice run
- [ ] Have water nearby (stay hydrated!)

---

## 💡 Tips for a Great Demo

### Camera & Audio
- **Good lighting**: Face a window or use desk lamp
- **Clear audio**: Use headset mic if possible
- **Eye contact**: Look at camera occasionally
- **Smile**: Be enthusiastic and confident!

### Screen Recording
- **Full screen browser**: Hide bookmarks bar (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + B)
- **Hide cursor clutter**: Close extra apps
- **Zoom level**: 100% for readability
- **Smooth scrolling**: Scroll slowly to showcase animations

### Speaking
- **Pace yourself**: Don't rush, speak clearly
- **Explain, don't just click**: Say what you're showing
- **Highlight unique features**: Focus on 3D elements, animations
- **Show personality**: Let your enthusiasm shine!

## 📤 After Recording
